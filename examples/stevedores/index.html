About
Wallace Investments: Your Trusted Stevedore for Conventional Cargo. With decades of expertise in the field, Wallace Investments is your preferred choice for the handling of conventional cargo. Our services cover everything from bulk cargo, heavy lift cargo, containers, and vehicles, and we ensure safe and secure handling of your shipment.
As the leading private operator at the Port of Auckland, we bring a family-oriented approach to our business, ensuring that your cargo receives the personalized care it deserves. Our advanced equipment and skilled personnel ensure efficient and safe loading and unloading of vessels, meeting the needs of both shippers and carriers.

health and safety
With a focus on reducing harm to the environment, employees, and stakeholders, Wallace invests in cutting-edge dust suppression technology and solutions. Additionally, the company provides training programs to enhance the efficiency and capabilities of its workforce.
Dedicated to improving safety management, Wallace has received certification from Telarc for ISO9001:2015, 14001:2015 & 45001:2018 standards. The company leads the way in stevedoring education by offering the NZQA-recognized New Zealand Certificate in Stevedoring to its employees and actively participating in the development of Maritime NZ submissions to the IMO.
