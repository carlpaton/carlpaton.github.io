Stick 25 Size

The HK link says its 1060mm but the wing I got is 1200 without the tips so I’ll go with the slightly longer size. These are some guestimates on wood to be used.

Fuselage

  • Ply re-enforcement where the landing gear attaches.
  • F1 -> 5 or 6mm ply
  • F2,3 -> 3mm ply
  • F4,5,6 -> 5mm balsa
  • Triangle stock -> Probably cut from 3mm balsa scraps
  • Gussets -> 2 or 3mm square
  • Sides -> 2mm balsa forward grain
  • Top/Bottom -> 2mm balsa sideways grain
  • Wing seats 2mm balsa vertical grain (Thanks for the tip bro Clive!)

Wing

  • Main spar & LE -> 8mm square hard balsa
  • Forward spar/stringers -> 4mm balsa
  • Sheer web -> 2mm balsa
  • Ribs & caps, 2mm balsa
  • Wing tips, 5 or 6mm balsa
  • Sheeting -> 2mm balsa
  • TE -> 6mm square (I think)
  • Triangle stock -> Probably cut from 3mm balsa scraps

Tail

  • 4mm balsa, I’ll build it up with 2mm to try save weight.

Mini Ultra Stick

980mm Span

E-Flight used to make these, I rebuilt one and over powered it with a park 480 and a 40 amp esc.

Fuselage

  • Park 450, 2200 3S, 10x7e prop
  • Overall Length: 34 in (865mm)
  • Flying Weight: 710g

Wing

  • Span: 38.75 in (985mm)
  • Area: 325 sq in (21 sq dm)
  • Estimated height TE to LE: (325 / 38.17 = 8.38 in) => 212mm (includes ailerons)
  • Ailerons throw 15mm Up/Down Low Rate, 19mm High Rate

Tail

  • Elevator throw 18mm Up/Down Low Rate, 23mm High Rate
  • Rudder throw 23mm Right/Left Low Rate, 32mm High Rate



References

  • https://www.horizonhobby.com/mini-ultra-stick-arf-efl2250
  • https://www.spektrumrc.com/Products/Default.aspx?ProdID=EFL2250
