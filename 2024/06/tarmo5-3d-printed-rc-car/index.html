Tarmo5 3d Printed RC Car

Thought this would be a fun project to build with my son, shout out to EngineeringNS for this open source projet.

3D Printed Parts

All the parts are here with instructions here copied from EngineeringNS

  • PLA for the main body parts
  • ABS for the shock risors, control/swing arms to the wheels and gears
  • TPU (95A SHORE HARDNESS) for the shafts, bumpers and connectors

Bill Of Materials

I listed them below but there is also a copy here from EngineeringNS

Electronics

  • D3542 1450KV Brushless Outrunner (its meant for a plane but clearly works)
  • Hobbywing 10BL120 Sensored 120A 2-3S Lipo Brushless ESC Speed Controller (need to find a 4s)
  • PDI-6221MG 20KG Digital Servo

Mechanical

  • 4 Wheels sets, between 100MM/120MM OD with 12mm Rim Drive Hex
  • 6802RS Bearing 15x24x5mm
  • 80mm Shock Absorber Oil Damper
  • 6mm steel balls for CV Joints x18
  • M3*350 rod x3
  • M3*8 cap screws x6
  • M3*12 cap screws x5
  • M3*16 cap screws x9
  • M3 nylock nuts x7
  • M4 nylock nuts x5
  • M4 washers x5
  • M4*20 cap screws x23
  • M4*40 cap screws x24
  • M4*12 cap screws x6
  • M4*35 hex head screws x4 (hub drivers)
  • Main Drive Gear Adaptor, 5mm ID 11mm OD shank (Stop Collar Ring Positioner)
  • Super Lube 92003 Silicone Lubricating Grease with PTFE

Radio Options

  • FLYSKY FS-GT2B GT2B 2.4G 3CH Radio Transmitter
  • Flysky FS-GT3B 2.4G 3CH Radio Model Remote Control
