Microwave maize bread

Baking bread has never been this easy. Within less than 30 minutes you can have a freshly baked maize bread.

Preparation time : 5 minutes Cooking time : 12-15 minutes Servings : 6

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 125 ml milk
  • 125 ml sugar
  • pinch salt
  • 250 ml maize meal
  • 30 ml cake flour
  • 7 ml baking powder
  • 1 can, cream-style
  • sweet corn
  • 1 can, whole-kernel

Method

Grease a 28 cm silicone loaf tin with nonstick spray.

  1. Whisk the eggs, milk, sugar and salt.
  2. Sift together the dry ingredients and mix with the egg mixture. Add the sweet corn and oil and mix well.
  3. Turn the batter into the tin and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Put the tin on an upturned saucer and microwave for 12-15 minutes or until done.
  5. Cool in the tin for 10 minutes before turning out on a wire rack.
  6. Serve hot or cold with butter and cheese.
